Litigation

Dispute Resolution/ Litigation

We represent litigants across a pleathora of Courts and Tribunals situated in Chandigarh New Delhi and Shimla. On Special request and for important matters, we also represent Clients in the District Courts of Punjab and Haryana.

Civil & Commercial Law practice

Expertise of our lawyers and the firm lies in Civil and Commercial Litigation. We have been in the profession for more than quarter of a century servicing our clients, arguing in Courts and ensuring results. Our Civil & Commercial practice spans across numerous practice areas including Arbitration Law, Banking & Debt Recovery Law, Commercial Law, Consumer Law, Election Laws, Electricity Laws, Environment Laws, Industrial Law, IPR, Land Laws, Maritime Laws, Matrimonial Laws, MACT, Conflict of Laws, Rent Laws, Reproductive Laws, Stock Exchange Arbitrations, Service Law, Technology Law and Town planning Laws We specialize in matters pertaining to Electricity Laws, Banking & Finance, Arbitration, Regulatory, Land & Revenue disputes, Rent matters. Service Disputes & Industrial Laws. We are equally adept at representing the Plaintiff/Appellant as well as the Defendant/Respondent. We have been successful in adopting a result-oriented approach and ensuring relief to our clients for the past 31 years. Please read about our practice areas

Constitutional Law practice

We have been arguing Constitutional matters and other matters of national importance for the past 31 years. We specialize in Writ petitions challenging the vires of statutes, writ petitions to safeguard constitutional rights especially right to equality, right to freedom of speech and expression, right to education, right against preventive detention, double jeopardy, right to property and right to life. We also specialize in Writ petitions safeguarding numerous legal and statutory rights. We believe in upholding the principles and values of the Constitution of India and in justice, equity and good conscience. We also represent petitioner in Public Interest Litigations (PILs) or disputes involving questions of substantial importance at general. We have been successful in adopting a result-oriented approach and in upholding the spirit of the Constitution for the past 31 years and will continue to do so.

Criminal Law practice

We have been practising criminal law for the past 31 years. We specialize in Socio-Economic offences and white collar crimes. We have managed to get our clients released on Bail (Regular as well as Anticipatory) from the High Courts in a number of matters. We have succeeded in having criminal complaints and FIRs against numerous accused persons including Company officials quashed. We have also succeeded in getting Order framing charges quashed. We also practice in the area of criminal contempt proceedings and special offences under the Information Technology Act. We are equally adept at representing the complainant as well as the accused and are regularly representing clients on issues relating to this field.
